About This Application

BinaryLoom Chat is an AI conversation platform that uses your Google account solely for authentication. We access your basic profile information (name, email) to create your account. Your conversations are processed by AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral) via Microsoft Azure and are never used to train AI models.

Data is stored in Sweden (EU) and you can delete your account and all data at any time.
